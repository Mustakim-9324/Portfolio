About Me
Designing a Webdite

I don’t know if I am passionate about being a web developer. I find it really easy to get involved in the projects I work on though. You get into the system of thought, find a way to build it/change it/fix it, and the release it to whoever needed it. It’s a fun cycle. It has plenty of headaches along the way, but overall, it’s a cool thing to do. I learn interesting tricks/tools/techniques along the way.

It’s an ever changing process. I prefer the though of adapting to new tricks as I go instead of having mastered some technique so thoroughly I’ll never have to put thought into it again except for odd situations.
It’s also providing an income to where my wife doesn’t have to work and can raise my daughters from home.
